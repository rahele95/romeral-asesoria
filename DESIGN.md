# El Romeral — Asesoría Virtual

## Brand Essence
Ultra-premium event venue. Reference the restraint, materiality and quiet luxury of Nobu Hotel and Benazuza. Generous negative space, slow elegant pacing, never crowded. Editorial, cinematic, hospitality-grade.

## Color Tokens
- --gold: #B8935A  (primary accent, headings highlight, CTAs, active states, thin hairline borders)
- --forest: #1A2F23 (secondary surfaces, cards)
- --forest-deep: #0D1A12 (page background, hero background)
- --ivory: #F5F1EB (primary text on dark, body copy)

Backgrounds are deep forest/near-black (#0D1A12). Text is ivory (#F5F1EB). Gold (#B8935A) is used sparingly for emphasis, never as a flood fill.

## Typography
- Headings / display: 'DM Serif Display', serif — elegant high-contrast serif. Large, airy, letter-spacing slightly tight. Use for titles like EL ROMERAL, prices, section numbers.
- Body / UI: 'Lato', sans-serif, weight 300 (light). Generous line-height (1.6+), wide letter-spacing for labels (uppercase, tracking ~0.15em).
- Decorative section numbers (01, 02...) are very large, thin, gold or low-opacity ivory.

## Shape & Spacing
- Minimal roundness (4px). Prefer sharp, refined edges.
- Hairline 1px borders in gold at low opacity.
- Lots of padding; sections breathe. Mobile-first, single column, tall layouts.

## Components
- Buttons: solid gold (#B8935A) with forest-deep text, OR ghost buttons with 1px gold border and gold text. Uppercase Lato, letter-spacing.
- Cards: forest (#1A2F23) surface, subtle gold hairline, generous padding.
- Inputs: transparent with bottom gold hairline border, ivory text, gold focus.

## Tone
Calm, confident, exclusive. No clutter, no bright colors outside the palette.
